Welcome to NgheGo! This guide walks you through the simplest way to start practicing: paste a YouTube link on the homepage and press "Bắt đầu". The app will prepare audio segments and an AI transcription so you can practice by listening and typing.

Tips:

- Choose short videos (2-5 minutes) to start.
- Use headphones for better listening accuracy.
- Replay difficult segments and slow down playback if needed.

Happy practicing!
